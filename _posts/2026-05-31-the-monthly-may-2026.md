May was the month the agentic-coding conversation matured past the hype: Simon Willison on the blurring line between vibe coding and real engineering, DeepMind's AlphaEvolve doing genuine scientific work, and a healthy run of "is this actually a good idea?" essays. On the open front, a reliability layer that drags an 8B model to near-perfect on agentic tasks, small open safety models, and a striking distillation of tool-calling into a 26M model.

**[An AI coding agent should reduce your maintenance costs](https://www.jamesshore.com/v2/blog/2026/you-need-ai-that-reduces-your-maintenance-costs)**
